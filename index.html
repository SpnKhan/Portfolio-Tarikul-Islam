Challenge

Results
  • ✓Reduced time-to-hire from 120 days to 65 days (46% improvement)
  • ✓Increased offer acceptance rate from 60% to 92%
  • ✓Saved $350K annually in recruiting costs
  • ✓Improved new hire satisfaction scores by 35%
